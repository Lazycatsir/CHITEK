Response Time

CHITEK AHF features a full response time of less than 50 microseconds (<50μs), enabling real-time harmonic compensation that responds instantly to dynamic load changes.

This ultra-fast response ensures that even rapidly fluctuating loads such as welding machines, elevators, and variable frequency drives are effectively compensated without delay, preventing harmonic buildup that could damage sensitive equipment.

Parallel Operation

Yes, CHITEK AHF units are designed for parallel operation. Up to 10 units can be paralleled without any communication cables, using a proprietary master-slave control algorithm.

Key benefits of parallel operation:

  • Capacity expansion from 30A to 3000A+
  • N+1 redundancy for critical applications
  • Automatic load sharing with droop control
  • Hot-swappable modules for maintenance without downtime

Parallel systems can be configured in less than 5 minutes through the intuitive front panel interface.

Communication Protocols

CHITEK AHF supports the following communication protocols for remote monitoring and control:

  • RS485 / Modbus RTU — standard on all models
  • Ethernet / Modbus TCP — optional interface module
  • Profibus DP — optional for industrial automation integration
  • Dry contact I/O — for simple start/stop and alarm signaling

All communication parameters (baud rate, parity, slave address) are configurable via the front panel keypad and display. CHITEK also provides a free PC-based monitoring software for data logging and trend analysis.

Lifespan and Maintenance

CHITEK AHF has a design life exceeding 15 years under normal operating conditions (ambient below 40°C, nominal load). Regular preventive maintenance is recommended every 12 months to ensure optimal performance.

  • DC bus capacitor replacement: every 8-10 years (recommended)
  • Cooling fan replacement: every 5-7 years (based on operating hours)
  • Air filter cleaning: every 3-6 months (dusty environments)
  • Firmware updates: available via CHITEK PC software tool

All CHITEK AHF products come with a standard 2-year warranty, with extended warranty options available up to 5 years.

Transformer Requirements

No, the CHITEK AHF does not require a dedicated isolation transformer. It connects directly to the low-voltage bus (380V-480V, 50/60Hz) through the existing circuit breaker or fused disconnect.

However, proper current transformer (CT) installation is critical for correct operation:

  • CTs must be installed on the mains side (grid source) of the load
  • CT ratio should be selected so the secondary current is 1A or 5A at full load
  • CT accuracy class 0.5 or better recommended for optimal performance
  • Standard CT wiring distance up to 100 meters with proper shielding

Harsh Environment Operation

CHITEK AHF is designed for reliable operation in demanding industrial environments:

  • Operating temperature: 0°C to +50°C (standard), with automatic derating above 40°C
  • Storage temperature: -25°C to +70°C
  • Humidity: 0-95% non-condensing
  • Ingress protection: IP20 standard, IP31 optional (NEMA 1 equivalent)
  • Altitude: up to 2000m without derating, up to 4000m with derating

For extreme environments (high dust, corrosive gases, outdoor), CHITEK offers custom enclosure options including forced ventilation, heat exchangers, and stainless steel construction.

Certifications and Compliance

CHITEK AHF products are fully certified and comply with major international standards:

  • CE (LVD + EMC) — compliance with EU directives for low voltage and electromagnetic compatibility
  • UL/cUL 508 — listed for industrial control equipment (optional)
  • IEC 61642 — harmonic filter application standard
  • IEC 61439-1/2 — low-voltage switchgear and controlgear assemblies
  • IEEE 519-2022 — recommended harmonic limits compliance
  • ISO 9001:2015 — quality management system (manufacturing facility)

Custom certification packages are available for specific regional or project requirements.

Commissioning and Configuration

CHITEK AHF is designed for quick and intuitive commissioning. The standard setup process takes less than 15 minutes and involves three simple steps:

  1. Auto-tuning: The AHF automatically detects line impedance and CT configuration with the press of a button
  2. Harmonic selection: Choose from pre-configured harmonic profiles or customize individual harmonic orders (2nd-50th)
  3. Verification: Built-in waveform display and harmonic spectrum analyzer confirm correct operation

For advanced configurations, CHITEK provides a free PC-based software tool for parameter backup/restore, data logging, and remote monitoring setup. Multi-unit parallel systems can be configured in under 5 minutes through the intuitive front panel interface without any external tools.

SVG vs Capacitor Banks

The fundamental difference is that SVG uses power electronics (IGBT/SiC) to inject or absorb reactive current in real time, while capacitor banks provide fixed-step compensation with mechanical switching.

Feature CHITEK SVG Capacitor Bank
Response Time <10ms 1-5 cycles (20-100ms)
Compensation Type Continuous, smooth Step-wise, discrete
Resonance Risk None (active injection) High (LC circuit formation)
Harmonic Filtering Built-in (hybrid models) Requires detuned reactors

Protection Features

CHITEK SVG includes comprehensive protection features to ensure safe and reliable operation:

  • Over-voltage / Under-voltage protection — automatic shutdown when grid voltage exceeds safe limits
  • Over-current protection — instantaneous trip on output over-current
  • Over-temperature protection — intelligent fan control with thermal shutdown
  • Short-circuit protection — IGBT module desaturation detection
  • Phase loss protection — detection and alarm for missing phases
  • Surge protection — built-in MOV for transient voltage suppression

All protection events are logged with time stamps and can be retrieved via the front panel display or remote communication interface for troubleshooting.

Lifespan and MTBF

CHITEK SVG has a design life exceeding 20 years under normal operating conditions (ambient temperature below 40°C, nominal load).

  • MTBF (Mean Time Between Failures): >150,000 hours
  • Electrolytic capacitor replacement: every 8-10 years (recommended)
  • Cooling fan replacement: every 5-7 years (based on operating hours)
  • IGBT module lifespan: >15 years under rated conditions

All CHITEK SVG products come with a standard 2-year warranty, with extended warranty options available up to 5 years. Regular preventive maintenance is recommended every 12 months for optimal performance.

Installation Requirements

CHITEK SVG is designed for straightforward indoor installation with the following requirements:

  • Mounting: Wall-mount (up to 100kvar) or floor-mount with base frame (above 100kvar)
  • Clearance: Minimum 200mm on all sides for ventilation and access
  • Ventilation: Ambient temperature 0°C to +50°C, forced air cooling standard
  • Electrical: Direct connection to LV bus via circuit breaker; CT installation on mains side
  • Cable entry: Bottom entry standard, top entry optional

For outdoor or semi-outdoor installations, CHITEK offers IP54-rated enclosures with built-in climate control.

Unbalanced Load Handling

Yes, CHITEK SVG features per-phase independent control, allowing it to handle severely unbalanced loads with ease.

Each phase is independently monitored and controlled, providing:

  • Individual phase-by-phase reactive power compensation
  • Automatic balancing of phase currents when used in conjunction with load balancing functions
  • Zero-sequence compensation for 4-wire systems (3-phase + neutral)
  • Seamless performance even when one phase has drastically different loading than others

This makes CHITEK SVG ideal for applications with unbalanced loads such as welding shops, data centers, and commercial buildings.

Renewable Energy Integration

CHITEK SVG is widely used in renewable energy applications to improve power quality and grid compliance:

  • Solar PV plants: Compensates reactive power fluctuations caused by cloud-induced irradiance changes
  • Wind farms: Provides voltage support during turbine startup and wind gusts
  • Energy storage systems: Maintains power factor during charge/discharge cycles
  • Microgrids: Stabilizes voltage in island mode operation

SVG units can be integrated with plant SCADA systems via Modbus TCP for coordinated reactive power management across the entire renewable facility.

Standby Power Consumption

CHITEK SVG is designed for high efficiency across all operating conditions, with minimal standby losses:

  • Standby power consumption: <2% of rated capacity (e.g., a 100kvar SVG consumes less than 2kW in standby)
  • Full-load efficiency: >98% at rated output
  • Sleep mode: When reactive power demand is below 5% of rated capacity for 10 minutes, the SVG enters sleep mode, reducing consumption to <0.5%
  • Automatic wake-up: The SVG instantly wakes from sleep mode when reactive power demand exceeds 10% of rated capacity

This intelligent power management ensures minimal energy costs during periods of low demand while maintaining instant readiness.

Communication Protocols

CHITEK SVG supports the following communication protocols for remote monitoring and control:

  • RS485 / Modbus RTU — standard on all models, supports multi-drop daisy-chain topology
  • Ethernet / Modbus TCP — optional interface module for network integration
  • Profibus DP — optional for industrial automation systems
  • Dry contact I/O — programmable inputs/outputs for start/stop, alarm, and status signaling

All communication parameters (baud rate, parity, slave address, IP configuration) are fully configurable via the front panel keypad and display. CHITEK also provides comprehensive PC-based monitoring software for data logging, trend analysis, and remote firmware updates.
