Shenzhen CHITEK New Energy Technology Co., Ltd., founded in 2019 and headquartered in Shenzhen, is a high-tech enterprise specializing in the research, development, manufacturing, and service of power electronic products. Committed to building an energy-efficient and green future.

INDUSTRY CASES

Industrial Manufacturing

Active harmonic filtering and power factor correction for heavy machinery, welding equipment, and motor-driven systems in factories.

Commercial & Medical Buildings

Protecting sensitive medical imaging equipment and building management systems from power disturbances and voltage sags.

Data Centers & Telecom

Ensuring zero-downtime power quality for critical IT infrastructure, UPS systems, and telecom switching stations.

Renewable Energy

Grid integration solutions for solar and wind farms, managing reactive power and harmonic distortion from inverters.

Transportation

Power quality solutions for EV charging stations, railway traction systems, and electric vehicle infrastructure.

Power Grid & Utility

Supporting grid stability with dynamic reactive power compensation and harmonic mitigation at substation level.
