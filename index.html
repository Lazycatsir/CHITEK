AHF

Active Power Filters covering 208V to 800V, with efficiency up to 99%. Full scene application coverage for industrial, commercial and utility applications.

Product Center →

SVG

CHITEK Static Var Generator employs advanced IGBT switching at up to 25.6kHz to precisely regulate inverter voltage magnitude and phase. Delivers dynamic reactive power compensation and three-phase load balancing with exceptional accuracy for demanding industrial applications.

Product Center →

AVR

CHITEK Automatic Voltage Regulator maintains output voltage within ±1% despite input swings up to ±30%. Utilizes advanced tap-changer and PWM control to shield critical equipment from voltage sags, surges, and brownouts across 208V–480V systems in unstable grid environments.
